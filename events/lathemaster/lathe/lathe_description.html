Lathe Master

“Knowledge is of no value unless it is put into practical use...!”

To enhance practical wisdom and to have an uncompromising learning experience..., TORQUE 19, is providing a showcase event LATHE MASTER
  • This is a cut throat competition where participants prove themselves !!
  • Participants are required to perform a set of operations on the job using lathe machine.
  • Problem statement will be given on the spot in the form of drawing.
  • The competition is to produce mechanical component in the drawing and the job done is evaluated based on dimensional accuracy, time and surface finish.
  • Drawing include several simple operations like facing, taper turning, knurling.
