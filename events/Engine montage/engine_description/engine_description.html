Engine Montage

"Its time to brace yourselves to prove to be a mechanical engineer.”

Join hands together to exhibit your talent in our TORQUE’19 Fest.

The event mainly includes the assembling of disassembled components of an internal combustion engine that are used in the automobile under the supervision of an experienced technician. A proper assembly of the engine plays vital role in an automobile industry because an improper assembly of engine components might lead to complete engine failure and severe consequences. In this event you can get hands on experience with some engine components and it helps in gaining practical knowledge about assembly of engine components which will helpful for industrial interface. The event consists of two rounds.
