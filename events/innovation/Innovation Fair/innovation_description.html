Innovation Fair

"Its time to brace yourselves to prove to be a mechanical engineer.”

Join hands together to exhibit your talent in our TORQUE’19 Fest.

Innovation fair is the unique initiative of the Department of Mechanical Engineering, JNTU Kakinada. It is a two day exhibition of innovative and creative products/devices to promote the spirit of innovation. It invites innovators from various colleges and organizations to showcase their out-of-the-box ideas, inventions, creations and working prototypes in the exhibition.

1. INNOVATIVE IDEA

  • Innovative ideas need to be explained along with the required documents of the idea.

2. INNOVATIVE PROTOTYPE

  • Participants whoever demonstrates an innovative prototype/working model can win the prizes.

3. INNOVATIVE PRODUCT

  • Innovative products are showcased and scrutinized by able judges.

Benefits of Participation:

  • A few of the best innovations may be provided with technical guidance for further improvement of the prototypes and working models.
  • Financial support may be granted out of the DIC funding for the deserving cases.
  • Patent guidance may be provided to the winners of the event.
  • All the participants will receive a certificate by the JNTU Kakinada.
  • Best innovative prototypes/products may get an opportunity to get displayed forever in the Innovation Research Building, JNTU Kakinada. Such products have the privilege of forwarding them to the national level and international level innovation exhibitions.

    • A maximum of four members are allowed per team.
